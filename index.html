Overall Performance Analysis

YouTube was the standout platform for March 2026, generating 13,920 total views across 13 videos. The video shoot completed this month gave the content an authentic edge — project showcase videos averaged over 1,900 views each and contributed the most to subscriber growth. The channel gained 10 new subscribers organically, the strongest month-on-month subscriber growth recorded for Bharath GRC on YouTube.

Facebook showed meaningful audience interaction this month, with the "Comment Your Doubts" video generating 370 views, 12 likes, and real comments from viewers asking about GRC materials — a clear sign that the content is reaching genuinely interested buyers and professionals.

Instagram maintained consistent daily posting with 2,613 total views across 20 posts, building the long-term foundation of brand recall and audience familiarity with the Bharath GRC name and product range.
